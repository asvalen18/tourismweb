LocalWays Bogotá

Discover the Heart of Colombia

Historical Routes

Plaza de Bolívar

I absolutely loved strolling around Plaza de Bolívar! It's the vibrant heart of Bogotá, surrounded by stunning government buildings that tell the city's rich history.

La Candelaria

Wandering through La Candelaria was like stepping back in time! The colonial architecture and cultural vibes made it an unforgettable experience.

Bogotá Cathedral

Visiting Bogotá Cathedral was awe-inspiring! Its religious significance and beautiful architecture left me in complete admiration.

Nature Routes

Monserrate

Climbing Monserrate was worth every step! The panoramic views of Bogotá from the top are simply breathtaking – a must-do!

Simón Bolívar Park

Simón Bolívar Park is a paradise for outdoor lovers! I had an amazing time relaxing and enjoying all the recreational activities there.

Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden was a delightful escape! Walking among the native Colombian plants felt like discovering a hidden treasure.

Museums

Gold Museum

The Gold Museum blew my mind! Seeing all those pre-Colombian gold artifacts up close was like touching history itself.

Botero Museum

Fernando Botero's art is pure genius! Spending time at his museum made me appreciate Colombian art in a whole new way.

Colón Theater

Attending a show at Colón Theater was magical! The performing arts here are world-class and truly captivating.

Traditional Food

Ajiaco

Ajiaco is comfort food at its best! This traditional Bogotá soup with chicken and potatoes warmed my soul on a chilly day.

Arepas

Arepas are a staple in my diet now! This corn-based delight is simple yet delicious – perfect for any meal.

Colombian Empanadas

These empanadas are addictive! The crispy exterior and juicy meat filling make them an irresistible snack.

Community Events

Rock al Parque

Rock al Parque is epic! As the largest free rock festival in Latin America, it had me rocking all night long.

Book Fair

The Book Fair was a book lover's paradise! Immersing myself in literature and culture was an enriching experience.

Ciclovía

Ciclovía is brilliant! Cycling through closed streets every Sunday made me feel part of Bogotá's vibrant community.
